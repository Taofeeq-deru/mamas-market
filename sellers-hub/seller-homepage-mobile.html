frequently asked questions

What can I sell on Mamas Market?

Mamas Market provides a marketplace for crafters, artists and collectors to sell their handmade creations, vintage goods (at least 20 years old), and both handmade and non-handmade crafting supplies.

How do fees work on Mamas Market?

Joining and starting a store on Mamas Market is free. There are three basic selling fees: a listing fee, a transaction fee, and a payment processing fee.

Listing fees are billed NGN 100, Its the same for all the states in Nigeria. But, this might differ in another country with another currency.

Offsite advertising is optional for most sellers, but may be required based on how much you make in sales on Mamas Market in a 12 month period. Visit our Help Center for more details on Offsite Ads fees.

How do I get paid?

Receive your money automatically in your registered bank/paypal account within 24 hours after daily or weekly sales end. Payment is made upon choosing which option suits you best, daily or monthly.

Do I need a debit or credit card to create a store?

No, a credit or debit card is not required to create a store. To be verified as a seller you have the choice to use either a credit card or to register via PayPal. You will not incur any charges until you open your store and publish your listings.
